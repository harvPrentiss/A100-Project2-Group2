About The Guild!

The Startup Guild exists to be a community site for the startup scene in Connecticut, and hopefully beyond in the future. This is a place to meet other like minded individuals or organizations in the startup ecosystem. The Startup Guild is a place where the community can thrive and grow by sharing knowledge and expereince. The Startup Guild has features for both individuals and companies.


    Companies/Organizations
  • Get your company out in the community spotlight
  • Post jobs on your companies profile page and get notified of interested parties
  • Find talented individuals looking for work in the startup community
  • Connect with other companies an organizations within the community

    Individuals
  • Get yourself in the community spotlight
  • Find job postings on company profiles or through the Startup Guild Twitter
  • Connect with other individuals in the startup community
  • Showcase your talents on your own profile
